Welcome to Kanha National Park

Nestled in the heart of Madhya Pradesh, Kanha National Park is one of India's largest and most well-preserved tiger reserves. The Khatia Gate serves as the primary entrance to this natural paradise, offering visitors the perfect base to explore the park's diverse wildlife and stunning landscapes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Kanha National Park?

The park is open from October to June, with winter months (November to February) offering the most pleasant weather. March to May provides better tiger sighting opportunities, though temperatures can be high.

Why choose Khatia Gate for your stay?

Khatia Gate is the most accessible entrance with excellent infrastructure and proximity to the core zone. It offers the widest range of accommodation options and better chances of wildlife sightings.
